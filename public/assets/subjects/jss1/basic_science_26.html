  3. It Reduces Anxiety and Stress: Regular activity relaxes nerves and cools the bran relieving stress.
  • The Body Systems fund best in a physically fit person.
  • It prevents Mental Decline — As we get older, our brain tends to lose some brain cells that are responsible for active learning. Exercises can help the brain fight such mental decline.

 

Unit 2

 Factors that Influence Physical fitness

There are a number of factors that influence physical fitness.

They - include the following;

  1. Type of Job: Most people have jobs where they sit most of the time. So, chances are limited for such people to be physically active. People in such sedentary jobs should help themselves by engaging in vigorous sports during their leisure.
  2. Ageing: Though physical fitness is relative, aging affects it, all the same organs such as the muscles, bones, heart, and so on, will no longer function optimally. Flexibility, agility, coordination will be reduced considerably.
  3. Health habit: The type of food one eats can influence one’s physical fitness
  4. Carbohydrate: - laden meals if eaten always and in large quantity, will always result to overweight which may give rise to obesity. Protein -deficient meals when taken exclusively for some time (especially children and even adults) may result in Kwashiorkor. These deficiency diseases or disorder obviously influence one’s physical fitness.
  5. Ignorance: Ignorance could be in the area of the type of food one takes or the type of activity one engages in or lack of the knowledge of activity or exercise one should engage in. Take for instance in the case of food, the quantity and quality, and the time to take it should be considered and so on.
  6. Environment: If one finds himself in an area where jogging or playing is seen as “idleness” and that it is better to be in the farm 24 hours, there is the likelihood that one’s physical fitness would be in jeopardy. However, if this same individual finds himself in an environment where a gymnasium is available, people are seen jogging and playing, laughing optimally. Flexibility, agility, and feeling happy, definitely, he will Coordination will be reduced join them. In this case one can say considerably. environment has both negative and positive influence on physical fitness depending on the environment one finds himself.
  7. Disease: Nothing can be more debilitating to the human body than diseases. Whenever any part of the body is sick the whole systems are affected — Circulatory system, muscular system, Skeletal system, respiratory system. This in tum, affects Participation in any physical activity. Sickness impedes movement of any type and so physical fitness without movement IS an aberration.
